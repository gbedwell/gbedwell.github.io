rigrag is an R package for identifying genes preferrentially targeted or avoided for HIV-1 integration relative to other genes. The integration frequency into each gene is compared to the expected integration frequency according to models of equally-sized random integration datasets. Genes with integration frequencies higher than random are called upper outliers or recurrent integration genes (RIGs) and genes with integration frequencies lower than random are called lower outliers or recurrent avoided genes (RAGs). Genes with integration frequencies indistinguishable from random are called non-outliers. The package includes functions to import the genic integration sites, to generate matched random models, to classify genes, to generate plots of the data, to calculate the cross-sample occurrence of RIG/RAG/non-outlier classification, to calculate the relative fraction of RIGs/RAGs/non-outliers in each sample analyzed, and others.
